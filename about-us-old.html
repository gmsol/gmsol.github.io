Kinetic Composites has been manufacturing carbon fiber composite products in the USA for over 25 years.

Our ability to produce the best quality carbon composites at affordable prices is unparalleled.

We have decided to take this expertise to the another level, producing our own line of carbon fiber boards under the name Kinetix.

Inspired by staff member Ryan Welch, an accomplished board rider in Orange County, Kinetic Composites has developed a line of boards manufactured using aircraft grade carbon fiber materials.

Most of our boards are 100% carbon fiber, not merely a single layer over the typical wood plank that has dominated the industry for years.

The use of carbon fiber in our boards brings into play all the benefits of carbon fiber: light weight, unsurpassed rebound properties and, surprisingly, a quieter board.

As added plus to an all-carbon board is superior resistance to moisture and other environmental effects, making it a board for all seasons.

We have total design control of the manufacture of these boards, including final thickness, weight and the ability to control the flexibility and rigidity of the board by adjusting internal layer direction and count to achieve the required flex properties.
