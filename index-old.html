From the days when "sidewalk surfers" first took their boards to the streets, to today's high energy international skate competitions, skateboarding has been in a constant state of evolution. But while the popularity and diversity of the sport have expanded, the boards themselves have not kept up with the advances. Decks from yesteryear were made using wood, as are most boards today. There has been some introduction of alternate materials such as bamboo, fiberglass overlay or even plastic.

Kinetic Decks is ready to make a bold move forward. We are proud to introduce our 100% carbon fiber longboard.

More than just a decorative layer over the usual wood deck, our boards are made from advanced carbon fiber composite materials from top to bottom.

Why do Formula 1 racecars and advanced aircraft extensively use carbon fiber? To get the best performance possible.

This lightweight but powerful material combines superior strength with great rebound properties, vibration dampening, desirable flex characteristics, and a surprisingly quiet ride. Our longboard also has superior resistance to moisture, making it a board for all seasons and any climate.

The strength and agility of our boards is a result of a carefully orchestrated production process. Using carbon fiber tape in the core of the boards allows our designers to choose the direction of the fiber in each layer, tailoring the flex and stiffness of the board for optimal performance.

You will find no better deck on the market. If you are looking to up your skateboarding game, try us. You won't be disappointed.
